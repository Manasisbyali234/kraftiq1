Our Story

KraftIQ was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical business solutions. Our journey began when Full Stack Developer Basavaraja and AI/ML Specialist Manasi S. Byali recognized the immense potential of combining engineering excellence with artificial intelligence.
